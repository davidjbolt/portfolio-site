About

I'm a Senior Sales Engineer at Delinea, specialising in Privileged Access Management (PAM) and identity security. I work closely with prospects and customers to understand their security challenges and design solutions that protect privileged credentials, control endpoint privileges, and secure remote access across on-premises and cloud infrastructure.

With a background spanning pre-sales, solution design, and technical enablement, I translate complex security concepts into clear business value — whether that's running a proof of concept, delivering executive-level demonstrations, or supporting a customer through onboarding and beyond.

Skills & Expertise

Privileged Access Management

Secret vaulting, credential rotation, just-in-time access, session recording and monitoring.

Endpoint Privilege Management

Least-privilege enforcement, application control, and policy-based privilege elevation on Windows and macOS.

Privileged Remote Access

Agentless remote access to servers, databases, and cloud workloads — without VPN exposure.

Identity & Cloud Security

Pre-Sales & Solution Design

Technical Enablement
